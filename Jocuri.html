Minecraft

Minecraft (from the English mine — "mine; mine" + craft — "craft; create") is an indie computer game in the sandbox genre created by Swedish programmer Markus Persson and released by his company Mojang AB. Persson published the initial version of the game in 2009; at the end of 2011, a stable version for Microsoft Windows PC was released, distributed through the official website
